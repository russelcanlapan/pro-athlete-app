Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 4, 2025 | Last Updated: September 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Pro Athlete ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Profile information and preferences

2.2 Protected Health Information (PHI)

  • Injury reports and medical history
  • Body measurements and fitness metrics
  • Training programs and exercise data
  • Progress tracking and performance analytics
  • Photos and videos related to fitness activities

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage patterns and app interactions
  • Log files and error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide fitness tracking, injury management, and coaching services
  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Safety: To ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Authorized Disclosures

  • Coaches: Athletes' health data may be shared with authorized coaches for training purposes
  • Healthcare Providers: With explicit consent, we may share data with medical professionals
  • Emergency Situations: We may disclose information to prevent serious harm or injury

4.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Google Firebase: Cloud hosting, authentication, and database services
  • Vercel: Web hosting and content delivery
  • All providers sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) as required by HIPAA

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures only authorized users can view your data
  • Audit Logging: All data access is logged for security monitoring
  • Regular Security Reviews: We conduct ongoing security assessments and updates
  • HIPAA Safeguards: Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards protect PHI

6. Your Rights

6.1 PIPEDA Rights

  • Right to know what personal information we have about you
  • Right to access and receive copies of your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to withdraw consent for data processing

6.2 HIPAA Rights

  • Right to access your protected health information
  • Right to request amendments to your health records
  • Right to an accounting of disclosures of your PHI
  • Right to request restrictions on uses and disclosures
  • Right to file complaints regarding privacy practices

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active and for legitimate business purposes
  • Account Deletion: Upon request, we will delete your account and associated data within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations
  • Backup Systems: Data in backup systems will be deleted according to our backup retention schedule

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or other approved mechanisms.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before taking effect. Your continued use of the service after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
